# 配图风格预设（按需选一种）

> 用于 `generate_image` 的 prompt 片段，确保风格一致。默认推荐 **A 极简信息卡片**。

## A. 极简信息卡片（默认）

- modern minimalist editorial infographic, clean grid layout, large bold Chinese typography, high contrast, generous whitespace
- background: solid light color or subtle paper texture
- color: 1 main accent color + black/gray text

## B. 清爽科技蓝

- modern tech editorial, blue accent, subtle gradient background, thin line icons, crisp UI-card style
- palette: navy / cyan / light gray

## C. 温暖生活方式

- lifestyle magazine layout, warm beige background, orange accent, friendly rounded sans-serif Chinese typography
- palette: beige / terracotta / dark brown

## D. 黑金高级感

- premium black and gold editorial poster, matte black background, gold accent lines, sophisticated typography
- 注意：小红书上黑底小字容易糊，务必大字号

## 建议的通用限制语（附加到prompt末尾）

- no watermark, no logos, no photo collage, no clutter
- ensure text is sharp and readable, avoid tiny text
